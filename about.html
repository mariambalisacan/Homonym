About Us

Mike Constantino

Managing Director

Hi! I'm Mike, better known as mikecons. You would be hard-pressed to find a hybrid, homonym like me in this market. Over the past 20 years, I have played pivotal roles in the worlds of advertising, marketing, strategy, digital, mobile, gaming and content - among many other fields. Most recently, I was the Managing Director for a global Content Marketing agency called Brand New Media and was previously the Country Sales Director of Yahoo in the Philippines. In all those 20 years, and even before then, I was also a consistently-busy musician. From as far back as I remember, as I worked on my career in business, I was also always writing, performing, creating, listening to, sharing music whether as a DJ, drummer, singer, songwriter, curator or consultant. All of the contacts I have nurtured in both the music and business fields for the past 2 decades puts me in a very unique position of finding synergies and bridging the gaps to give artists and creators a new way to earn from their art – while at the same time, giving brands that much-needed authentic content that they can use to incite their target markets.

Christel Boncan-Constantino

Managing Partner

Hi! I'm Christel – Mike's other half, in life and in business. I come from a family of serial entrepreneurs and am hardwired to think like one when it comes to starting businesses. In the last 10 years, I have worked in the arenas of event management, publishing, advertising, marketing and brand building and had an unforgettable stint as a radio DJ for one of the top FM stations in Manila. I have built my own independent lifestyle and clothing brands; and I am also a creative and a writer at heart, having written for magazines, broadsheets and online properties.


Here in Manila, we have a burgeoning creative economy and community that I am blessed to be a part of. As such, we are in a unique position to activate and engage these bright young stars to partner with us in creating and producing some of the most engaging and authentic music and content we have yet to see.
